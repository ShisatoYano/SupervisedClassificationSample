## 1 dimensional input
Input  
$$
    \bm{X} = \left(
        \begin{array}{c}
            x_0 \\
            x_1 \\
            \vdots \\
            x_{N-1}
        \end{array}
    \right)
$$
Label vector
$$
    \bm{T} = \left(
        \begin{array}{c}
            t_0 \\
            t_1 \\
            \vdots \\
            t_{N-1}
        \end{array}
    \right)
$$
